SAY Hi.

The website started as personal project during the Covid-19 pandemic and the “New York on Pause” stay-at-home order. I spent my time in quarantine studying: Agile project management, UX/UI design, and front-end web development. The site is intended to showcase what I’ve learned and what I can do.

What is "Noble-Pursuits?" I mean, come on. What Millennial doesn’t dream of starting their own lifestyle brand, right??? At least that was my intention in 2017 when I snagged the @noble_pursuits Instagram handle (Oh, 2017… those were simpler times). In 2020 Noble-Pursuits is a venue for me to celebrate the hard work and dedication it takes to create something that can inspire others; a platform to dissect what makes a design good or art impactful or music moving and to discuss how these objects and artifacts, the artists and artisans enrich society.
